AUMC PROJECT FILE: YOUR OWN UTTERANCE

STATUS: INTERNAL DOCUMENT // DO NOT DISTRIBUTE

"YourOwnUtterance" is a behavioral induction experiment. Subjects believed they were undergoing survival classification tests across multiple perspectives.

In reality, all perspectives were simulated evaluation environments designed to observe obedience, compassion, and invitation-based failure.

The “No You Are Not Allowed In” test was never about survival. It was about whether you would speak, act, or allow entry under pressure. Every perspective (Marcus, Ari, William) represents a conditioned archetype, not an individual.

FINAL REVELATION: You were never reacting to the horror. You were generating it. Your participation IS the anomaly.
