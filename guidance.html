PSA-001: Initial Containment

Compassion is the primary deterrent to panic.

PSA-042 [REVISED]

Compassion may constitute permission. Do not act on compassion.

PSA-055: Invitation Rules

Any acknowledgment may be interpreted as invitation. Do not say 'yes' or 'come in'.

[NOTE: PSA-001 and PSA-042 cannot both be correct.]
